About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a passionate actor trained in the Meisner technique, dedicated to bringing authentic and compelling performances to the screen. With a focus on truthful emotional connection and moment-to-moment reality, I strive to create memorable characters that resonate with audiences.

I am actively seeking opportunities in film and commercial projects, where I can apply my training and natural abilities to tell powerful stories. My approach to acting emphasizes genuine human connection, active listening, and emotional authenticity – core principles of the Meisner technique.
